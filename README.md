# Expense Tracker Offline

A powerful, secure, and offline-first personal finance application offering simplified expense tracking, detailed insights, debt management, and shopping organization.

### üìä Comprehensive Expense Tracking
- **Quick Add**: Easily add expenses and income with amounts, categories, dates, and notes.
- **Categorization**: Organize your spending with intuitive categories and customizable icons.
- **Recurring Transactions**: Set up **Weekly** or **Monthly** recurring transactions that automatically generate on schedule.
- **Shortcuts**: Create shortcuts for frequent expenses to add them in a single tap from the menu.
- **Transaction Management**: Edit, delete, and view a history of all your financial activities.

- **Monthly Budgets**: Set spending limits for specific categories or your total monthly expenditure.

- **Dashboard Integration**: Quick access to debt overview directly from the main screen.

- **Multi-Period View**: Analyze your spending with **Weekly**, **Monthly**, and **Yearly** reports.

- **CSV Export**: Export your entire financial history to CSV for external analysis or backup.

- **Manual Backups**: Create local backup files of your database and restore them anytime.

- **Smart Empty States**: Helpful prompts and illustrations when you're just getting started.
